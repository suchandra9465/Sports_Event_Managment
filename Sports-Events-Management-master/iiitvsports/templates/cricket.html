CRICKET

ABOUT US

Cricket is a passion in India and we, as a team represent the passion of IIIT Vadodara towards this legendary game. Cricket is in our hearts and our souls. The IIITV cricket team sincerely believes in qualities such as true sporting spirit and team effort. Each and every member of the team is a reflection of these values. The team comprises of a blend of cricket enthusiasts instilled with inborn talents and all these players gel together to form this fantastic unit.

FACILITIES

We have a well-devoted ground especially for cricket with the facilities of flood lights and nets. the cricket ground, is the Main Ground near the Academic Area . The pitch is regularly maintained by experienced people. These facilities are mostly for institute team players.
